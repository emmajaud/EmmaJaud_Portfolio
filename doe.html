THE PROBLEM

My team and I wanted to figure out why the Department of Energy website is not approachable to the common user looking to find information on energy resources in the U.S.

OUR SOLUTION

Our team worked together to analyze and eventually rebuild the energy.gov website to make it more accessible and useful for new users. Our goal was to educate users about the uses of energy, but not overwhelm them with information.
